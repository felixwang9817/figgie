First, read the rules here: Figgie Game Rules

Once 4 players are loaded, press Start Game to receive your cards and start trading.

You can enter the following commands:

  • SUIT at X to make an offer, e.g. spades at 10. You must have a card of SUIT to sell.
  • X bid for SUIT to make a bid, e.g. 5 bid for hearts. This will trade if there's currently an offer of equal or lower price.
  • take SUIT to buy SUIT at the current best offer price. Only valid if there's an offer.
  • sell SUIT to sell SUIT at the current best bid price. Only valid if there's a bid.
  • clear or out to clear all your bids and offers.

The market always clears after a trade happens.

Press End Game to end trading and calculate rewards.
